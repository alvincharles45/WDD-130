Explore Our Rivers

Discover stunning destinations and world-class whitewater rafting adventures guided by our experienced team.

Main Fork of the Salmon River, Idaho

Nestled deep in the Frank Church Wilderness of No Return, the Salmon River is famous for its scenic beauty, wildlife, and exciting rapids ranging from Class I–IV. Families love the warm, slower late summer waters for swimming and camping.

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Flowing through Utah’s dramatic desert cliffs, Desolation Canyon is a National Landmark with stunning views, historic sites, and fun rapids. It’s perfect for a 6-day trip for families and groups looking for adventure and quiet beauty.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

The Grand Canyon offers world-class whitewater combined with breathtaking geology and hidden side canyons. Explore ancient ruins, thrilling rapids, and make memories on the adventure of a lifetime with Dry Oar Rafting.
