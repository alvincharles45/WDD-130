Weekly Workout Routine

Workout Plans

Day 1: Full Body Strength

  • Squats – 4×10
  • Push-ups – 3×15
  • Bodyweight/Dumbbell Deadlifts – 3×10
  • Overhead Press – 3×10
  • Plank – 3×30 sec

Day 2: Core & Cardio

  • Jumping Jacks – 3×1 min
  • Bicycle Crunches – 3×20
  • Mountain Climbers – 3×30 sec
  • Russian Twists – 3×20
  • High Knees – 3×1 min

Day 3: Lower Body Burn

  • Walking Lunges – 3×20
  • Glute Bridges – 3×15
  • Step-ups – 3×10/leg
  • Wall Sit – 3×45 sec
  • Calf Raises – 3×20

Day 4: Active Recovery

  • Child’s Pose – 2 min
  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 10 rounds
  • Hamstring Stretch – 1 min/leg
  • Neck Rolls – 10 each dir.
  • Light Walk/Yoga Flow – 15–20 min
